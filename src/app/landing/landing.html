About StrayRescue

StrayRescue is a comprehensive, real-time platform designed to streamline the animal rescue process. Our main features include instant reporting of animals in distress via a mobile-friendly interface, efficient coordination of volunteers based on location and availability, and transparent tracking of each rescue case from report to resolution. We empower communities to act quickly and effectively, ensuring that every animal gets the chance for a safe and loving home.

How It Works

1. Report Animal

Submit a report with location and details of an animal in need.

2. Volunteer Assigned

The nearest available volunteer is notified and assigned to the case.

3. Rescue in Progress

The volunteer provides real-time updates as they rescue the animal.

4. Rescue Completed

The animal is safely secured and transported for care.
